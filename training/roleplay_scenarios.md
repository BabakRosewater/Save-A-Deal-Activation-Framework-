# Roleplay Scenarios — Save-A-Deal Activation

Use these scenarios in training to practice the framework end-to-end.

---

## Scenario 1 — Payment-Sensitive SUV Shopper

**Profile**

- Name: John
- Situation: Needs SUV for growing family + occasional towing.
- Concern: Monthly payment; wants to compare to current vehicle.

**Objectives**

- Practice making a specific in-store promise.
- Send taillight text and same-day email (role-play).
- Enter salesperson and manager CRM notes.

**Prompts for Trainer**

- Ask salesperson to role-play the **end of the visit**:
  - Guest is leaving without buying.
  - Salesperson must:
    - Recap motivator.
    - Make a specific promise.
    - Confirm timeline.

---

## Scenario 2 — Research-Heavy Guest, Not Ready Today

**Profile**

- Name: Sarah
- Situation: Researching compact SUV vs sedan; early in process.
- Concern: Not rushed; wants to “think about it.”

**Objectives**

- Practice not pushing for a decision.
- Use promise + gratitude to keep the door open.
- Capture motivators and timeline accurately in CRM.

---

## Scenario 3 — Trade-In Focused Guest

**Profile**

- Name: Mike
- Situation: Wants to upgrade but unsure about trade value.
- Concern: “I want to see what my truck is really worth first.”

**Objectives**

- Make a promise around trade evaluation or follow-up numbers.
- Use next-day promise fulfillment email + text.
- Set up a natural return visit.

---

For each scenario, run the following:

1. **In-Store Conversation** (2–3 minutes)
2. **Taillight Text** (pick wording)
3. **Same-Day Email** (outline or draft)
4. **Salesperson CRM Note** (fill template)
5. **Manager CRM Note** (fill template)
6. **Next-Day Call Opener** (role-play)
