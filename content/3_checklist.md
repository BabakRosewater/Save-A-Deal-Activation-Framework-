# Taillight Follow-Up & Save-A-Deal Activation Checklist

> **Follow-up happens before the customer leaves.**

---

## 1️⃣ Before the Guest Leaves — Make a Specific Promise

- ☐ Confirm their **motivator** (why they’re shopping / problem they’re solving)
- ☐ Make a **clear promise**:
  - What you’ll send (info, options, records, etc.)
  - When you’ll send it (e.g., “by 12:00 p.m. tomorrow”)
- ☐ Confirm best **email** and **mobile** number

---

## 2️⃣ Taillight Text — Gratitude Only

- ☐ Send a short **thank you text** shortly after they leave
- ☐ No selling, no pressure
- ☐ Optional: reference the promise and timing

---

## 3️⃣ Same-Day Email — Gratitude + Recap + Links + Promise

- ☐ Subject line: thank you / recap (not “urgent” or “deal”)
- ☐ Open with **genuine gratitude**
- ☐ Recap:
  - Vehicle(s) they looked at
  - What matters most to them
- ☐ Include relevant links:
  - Vehicle
  - Credit app
  - Trade appraisal
- ☐ Restate the **promise** (what + when)
- ☐ Reassure: “I’m here as a resource, no pressure”

---

## 4️⃣ CRM Notes — Salesperson + Manager

**Salesperson Notes**

- ☐ Motivator & problem they’re solving
- ☐ Vehicles considered and why
- ☐ Numbers presented (price/payment/down/term/trade)
- ☐ Objections or concerns
- ☐ Promises made (what + when)
- ☐ Timeline to decide

**Manager Notes**

- ☐ Manager intro recap
- ☐ Read on urgency, decision-makers, and deal temperature
- ☐ Manager commitments
- ☐ Recommended next-day strategy

---

## 5️⃣ Next-Day Promise Fulfillment + Text Ping

- ☐ Send the promised information by the agreed **time**
- ☐ Immediately send a short **text**:
  - “As promised, I just sent you an email with…”
  - Invite them to review and mention you’ll call
- ☐ Make the **call** when you said you would

> Every promise we keep builds trust.  
> Every clean note we write makes tomorrow’s Save-A-Deal stronger.
